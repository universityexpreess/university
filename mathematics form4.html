Mathematics form 4 Topical Questions and Answers
longitudes and latitutes
  1. Define longitudes and latitudes. Explain their significance in geography and navigation.
  2. Differentiate between longitudes and latitudes in terms of their measurement and orientation on the Earth's surface.
  3. What is the Prime Meridian? Describe its role in determining longitudes globally.
  4. Explain the concept of parallels of latitude and meridians of longitude. How are they numbered and labeled?
  5. Calculate the distance in degrees between two points on the same meridian if the difference in latitude is 30 degrees.
  6. Given a map with coordinates (30°N, 40°E), identify the hemisphere and the quadrant where this point is located.
  7. Discuss the effects of Earth's curvature on longitudes and latitudes, especially near the poles and equator.
  8. Determine the time difference between two locations situated at longitudes 60°W and 120°E if the standard time difference per 15° longitude is 1 hour.
  9. Explain the concept of time zones and how they are related to longitudes.
  10. Discuss the challenges and solutions related to using longitudes and latitudes for accurate geographical measurements and calculations.
